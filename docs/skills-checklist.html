Skills checklist

task (10 points each) item completed correctly?
YSI calibration followed SOP to calibrate DO, conductivity, and pH (2-point calibration)
waited for readings to stabilize before accepting calibration
Program and deploy continuous data logger Followed SOP to program continuous HOBO datalogger to deploy at desired date, time and sampling interval using HOBO Connect phone app
Securely attached logger to block for underwater deployment
Securely attached line to block and buoy to line using proper knots (bowline and lobster buoy hitch)
Filtering for TSS Followed SOP to filter water samples for TSS
Wore gloves
Used pre-muffled filters for TSS
Recorded volume filtered
Rinsed filter with DI water before removing filter from filter funnel
Folded filter in half with filtrate facing in before placing in foil
Labeled foil with name, date, location, filter number, and volume filtered
Basic water quality measurements Followed SOP to use YSI, secchi disk, and LiCOR to measure water quality, secchi depth, and a vertical light profile
Waited for YSI readings to stabilize before recording data
Accurately transcribed YSI, secchi disk, and light data to water quality data sheet
Recorded researcher names, date, and location on water quality data sheet
Properly secure boat Properly secured boat to dock using a cleat hitch and clove hitch+half hitch
